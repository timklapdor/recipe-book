Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 4 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 beaten eggs

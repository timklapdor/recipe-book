Chicken & Leek Casserole

Flavoursome and morish one pot dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs plain flour
  • 8 chicken thigh fillets, trimmed, halved crossways
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 leek, halved, washed, sliced
  • 4 rashers bacon, chopped
  • 2 tsp crushed garlic
  • 2 tsp chopped ginger
  • 2 tsp lemon grass
  • 1l chicken stock
  • ½ cup white wine
  • 100g button mushrooms, sliced
  • 100g green beans, trimmed, halved
  • 1 cup risoni

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C
  2. Add leek, bacon and garlic to pan. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 to 4 minutes or until leek is tender.
  3. Add chicken and cook till sealed.
  4. Add 2 wine and mushrooms.
  5. Add flour and coat for 2 minuets.
  6. Add stock and bring to the boil. Remove from heat. Cover and bake for 40 minutes.
  7. Remove cover and add risoni. Return to oven for 10 minutes.
  8. Add beans, cover and cook for a further 10 minutes.
  9. Serve

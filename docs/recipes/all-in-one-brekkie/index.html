Ingredients

  • 6 knackwurst sausage
  • 6 large portobello mushrooms, ends trimmed
  • 2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 bacon rashers
  • 6 ripe roma tomatoes, halved lengthways
  • 6 eggs
  • Toast, buttered, to serve

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Arrange the sausages and mushrooms in a large roasting pan. Drizzle over oil and season with salt and pepper. Bake in the oven, turning once, for 10 minutes.
  3. Add the bacon and tomato and bake for a further 10 minutes or until mushrooms and tomato are soft.
  4. Arrange the mixture in the pan to create 6 spaces. Crack 1 egg into each space.
  5. Bake for 4-5 minutes or until the eggs are cooked to your liking.
  6. Serve immediately with buttered toast, if desired.
